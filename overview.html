Project Overview

Addressing critical healthcare access barriers for vulnerable populations

The Problem

From our research, we identified four major challenges refugees and undocumented immigrants face when seeking healthcare:

  • Lack of awareness of free healthcare programs
  • Confusion about eligibility based on legal status
  • Language barriers
  • Fear of deportation

Our Solution

LifeCare addresses these challenges through three key features:

  • Find Nearby Free Healthcare Services
  • Eligibility Checker
  • Easy Appointment Booking

Target Users

Our primary users are refugees and undocumented immigrants who:

  • Need healthcare services
  • Have limited financial resources
  • May have language barriers
  • Are concerned about privacy
