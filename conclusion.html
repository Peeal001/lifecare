Reflections & Conclusion

Lessons learned and future directions

What Worked Well

  • Simple, step-by-step process for finding and booking healthcare
  • Privacy-focused design that builds trust with vulnerable users
  • Multilingual support to overcome language barriers
  • Clear eligibility checker that reduces confusion

Improvements

  • Could implement user profiles to reduce repetitive data entry
  • Additional language support for less common languages
  • More comprehensive help system with in-app guidance
  • Integration with more healthcare provider systems

Future Directions

  • Expand to include additional social services beyond healthcare
  • Partner with refugee support organizations for wider adoption
  • Add community features for peer support
  • Develop mobile app version for greater accessibility

Final Thoughts

LifeCare successfully addresses critical barriers to healthcare access for refugees and undocumented immigrants. Through our human-centered design process, we've created a solution that is not only functional but also sensitive to the unique needs and concerns of our target users. The heuristic evaluation provided valuable insights that helped us refine our prototype, though there remain opportunities for further improvement and expansion.
